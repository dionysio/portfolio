July 2019 -

Zenkraft - Multi-Carrier Shipping Integration

Zenkraft provides shipping, tracking and returns directly in Salesforce. 100% native apps used by over 50,000 users and over 20% of Fortune 500 for shipping booking, tracking, rating and much more on Salesforce™.

Initially, I was hired to migrate the codebase from Google App Engine to Heroku. After this initial migration was done, we've agreed on continuing the work, consisting mostly of integrating additional shipping carriers.

Shipping carriers are usually older companies with complex and legacy systems, so their APIs are less than ideal. Every carrier has their own API and while their function is virtually the same (create a shipment, return a label, schedule a pickup or track an existing shipment), the way they achieve these few methods differ vastly. The companies we work with typically don't have resources to integrate individual carriers and keep an eye on the integrations. So we offer one unified API, which provides a middle man from Salesforce to <insert carrier>. I'm the person who implements new carrier integrations & maintains all the old ones on the backend.

Homer - Text Analysis Tool

Homer can help make your text more clear, simple and useful for the reader. Homer provides information on an overall text as well as on individual paragraphs and it gives insights into readability, length of paragraphs, length of sentences, average sentences per paragraph, average words in a sentence, etc. Homer also tries to identify certain kind of vague words as well as tracking the frequency of "and" words in the text.

For me, this was a chance to learn VueJS and combine it with my backend expertise to provide a whole solution.
